1. Overview

Promptle ("we", "us", or "our") operates the web application at promptle.unr.dev. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have. By using Promptle you agree to these practices.

2. Information We Collect

Account data

When you sign in through Auth0 we receive your email address, display name, and profile picture URL. We store these in our database to power your profile and track your game activity.

Game data

We store game history, scores, saved games, and custom topics you submit. Topics you enter for AI-generated games are sent to OpenAI's API to produce puzzle content.

Payment data

Subscription and token purchases are processed by Stripe. We never see or store your full card number. We receive a Stripe customer ID and subscription status from Stripe's systems.

Usage data

Our server logs standard request metadata (IP address, browser type, pages visited) for security and operational purposes. Logs are retained for a limited period and are not sold or shared with advertisers.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To operate and improve the Promptle game experience
  • To authenticate your account via Auth0
  • To process payments and manage your subscription or token balance via Stripe
  • To generate AI puzzle content via OpenAI (your topic text only — no personal data is sent)
  • To detect and prevent abuse, fraud, or security incidents
  • To respond to support requests

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties.

4. Third-Party Services

We rely on the following third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policies:

  • Auth0 — identity and authentication (auth0.com/privacy)
  • Stripe — payment processing (stripe.com/privacy)
  • OpenAI — AI puzzle generation (openai.com/policies/privacy-policy)

5. Cookies and Local Storage

We use browser local storage to save your theme preference and in-progress game state. Auth0 uses cookies to maintain your login session. We do not use tracking or advertising cookies.

6. Data Retention

We keep your account and game data for as long as your account is active. You can delete your account at any time from your profile page, which removes your data from our systems. Stripe may retain payment records as required by law.

7. Children's Privacy

Promptle is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can delete it.

8. Your Rights

You can access, update, or delete your account data via your profile page at any time. If you have additional questions or requests about your data, please contact us.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "last updated" date above. Continued use of Promptle after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
